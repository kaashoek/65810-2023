<h1>6.5810 (F2023): Operating Systems Research Seminar: Serverless computing</h1>

<p>6.5810 is a graduate-level seminar-style class focused on research
in operating systems. It assumes you have
taken <a href="https://pdos.csail.mit.edu/6.S081/2022/">6.1810</a>
or an equivalent undergrad OS class.  The topic of this year's seminar
is serverless computing; so, if you have
taken <a href="https://pdos.csail.mit.edu/6.824/">6.5840</a>, that is
ok too. Each student will lead discussion of one or more papers, and
design and build a substantial project in the area of serverless
computing.  See <a href="logistics.html">logistics</a> for the format
of the class.
